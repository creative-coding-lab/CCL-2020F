This is a group software project to develop a screen-based interactive experience utilizing Object-Oriented Programming concepts and mouse and/or keyboard interaction(s). Students are encouraged to explore diverse methods of visualizing/manipulating "objects" and find their own ways how they effectively interact with users. The project needs to discuss effective and/or meaningful interaction methods and demonstrate a coherent understanding of Object-Oriented Programming and technical proficiency in p5.js.

Students will create a sketch that is an inspiration from / application of the techniques and concepts addressed by the midterm date.
